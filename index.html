The Greatest Bookshop in the World

Our Bookshop is now an online bookstore with a mission to financially support local, independent bookstores. We believe that bookstores are essential to a healthy culture. They’re where authors can connect with readers, where we discover new writers, where children get hooked on the thrill of reading that can last a lifetime. They’re also anchors for our downtowns and communities.

Harry Potter
Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows is a rousing culmination of seven books' worth of page-turning plot

It doesn't rely solely on its previous installments to wow you. There are new characters, new dangers, and new twists to keep you guessing. It's a satisfying conclusion to a much-loved series.

Review: JK Rowling finished the groundbreaking series with a remarkable resolution. Harry Potter and The Deathly Hallows concludes all the loose ends and plot points, leaving just enough mystery to allow the reader to feel satisfied yet still desiring for more in the perfect way with.

Character List: Harry Potter, Hermoine Granger, Ron Weasley, Albus Dumbledore, Lord Voldemort, Severus Snape, Draco Malfoy, Luna Lovegood, Ginny Weasley, Remus Lupin, Neville Longbottom, Alastor Moody, Bellatrix Lestrange, Dobby, Rubeus Hagrid, Minerva McGonagall, Molly and Arthur Weasley, Dolores Umbridge, Lucious Malfoy, Bill Weasley, Fleur Delacour, Aberforth Dumbledore, Nymphadora Tonks, Xenophillius Lovegood, Hedwig, Dudley Dursley, Peter Pettigrew, Percy Weasley, Mr. Ollivander and Many Members of Dumbledore's Army, The Order Of the Phoenix. Also Many Death Eaters and Countless number of Other Characters.

Settings: Number 4, Privet Drive, Number 12, Grimmauld Place, Hogwarts, Hogsmeade, Forest of Dean, Godric's Hollow, The Burrow, Gringotts, Diagon Alley, Bill and Fleur's House and so many more.

Part 1 Plot: Without the guidance and protection of their professors, Harry, Ron and Hermione begin a mission to destroy the Horcruxes, the sources of Voldemort's immortality. Though they must rely on one another more than ever, dark forces threaten to tear them apart. Voldemort's Death Eaters have seized control of the Ministry of Magic and Hogwarts, and they are searching for Harry -- even as he and his friends prepare for the ultimate showdown.

Part 2 Plot: A clash between good and evil awaits as young Harry, Ron and Hermione prepare for a final battle against Lord Voldemort. Harry has grown into a steely lad on a mission to rid the world of evil. The friends must search for the Horcruxes that keep the dastardly wizard immortal. Harry and Voldemort meet at Hogwarts Castle for an epic showdown where the forces of darkness may finally meet their match.
